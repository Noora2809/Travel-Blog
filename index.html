yo! i'm Jeffy

I’m an American solo female traveler living out of a backpack since 2016, traveling to all kinds of places many tourists never consider. Cultures call to me, maps lie to me, and I’ll go anywhere so long as there are friendly faces and wild spaces. My goal is to inspire you to question stereotypes, travel responsibly… and see how girls can travel anywhere.
